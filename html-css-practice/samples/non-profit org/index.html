Education for change

Kenya Make a Difference supports the education of over 500 children in Kenya.

Make a Difference Onlus is a non-profit organization founded in 2008 by the Chilver and Paloschi families, with the aim of supporting the education of unprivileged children living on the Eastern Coast of Kenya.

Help Us Obtain our Objective

Over the years, our association has focused on supporting the education of over 1000 children in Kenya. The intent is to increase the children's level of education and to introduce new students each year into the program.
